ℹ️ About this Project

🎯 Problem Statement 3

Transform 6 camera images into a 2D Bird's-Eye-View occupancy grid showing obstacles and free space for Level 4 autonomous driving systems.

🧠 Architecture

ResNet50 + FPN backbone → LSS view transformer → FastOcc 2D FCN decoder → Occupancy head

📊 Training Results

Occupancy IoU: 0.2081
Epochs: 20 | Device: T4 GPU
Dataset: nuScenes v1.0-mini

📚 Research Papers

1. "Delving into the Devils of BEV Perception" — IEEE TPAMI 2023

2. "FastOcc: Accelerating 3D Occupancy Prediction" — ICRA 2024
